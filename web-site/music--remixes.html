    Nude (The Noise Floor Music Rebuild) — Piano Version
  2. Duration: 4:09
  3. Year: 2008
  4. Genre: Electronic
  5. Download: Score
  6. Download: Audio

In the spring of 2008, Radiohead announced a remix contest for their song Nude. Anyone could pay for the stems to the song on iTunes, and voting would close on 1 May.

Somehow, I was not made privy to this information until mid-April, which was very late in the contest timeline. With little time to do any true innovative remixing, I got to work on writing a complimentary piano melody for the outro of the song.

From concept to submission took a week, and I even made a video for the remix, releasing it a day after my submission. It’s available at https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HYRwzAk1DpE.

Although I knew I wouldn’t win, I was happy with the result, and ecstatic with the beautiful accompanying melodic line of the outro. To perform it live, I transcribed it to guitar later that spring.

Recorded at Hunter College and my home studio in Brooklyn, April 2008.
